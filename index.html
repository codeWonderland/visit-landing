IMAGINE:
... one of the most stunning and expansive views of natural scenic beauty from any campus in the U.S.
... dorm rooms that are too cool and unique to be called “dorms.” Our first-year residence halls are all Victorian-era mansions, with no two rooms the same—all equipped with ultra fast Wi-Fi—and all just steps to downtown Burlington and Lake Champlain’s waterfront.
... a small community that nurtures big, game-changing ideas and prizes authenticity and individuality.
... a student lifestyle that’s humming with energy: with more than 60 clubs to join, like Ski & Ride, Robotics and Equestrian, you may never have enough time to do it all, but you’ll have a blast trying!
... a city that lives up to its hype as the “#1 best college town” ... that’s as recognized for its amazing technology culture (named “White House Techhire City”; “Top City for Tech Innovation”, etc.) as it is for its commitment to local farmers markets and sustainable practices (it was the first city in America to run completely on renewable energy).

INFO SESSION & CAMPUS TOUR

Join us at our Perry Hall Welcome Center to learn more about our unique academic approach, life as a Champlain student, and our admission & financial aid process. After the information session you’ll go on a small-group walking tour of campus led by one of our Student Ambassadors which will give you a wonderful chance to see what being a student at Champlain is like.

SUMMER OPEN HOUSE: FRIDAY, JULY 13, 2018

Summer Open House is one of the best (and the most fun) ways to see if who we are, and how we do things differently, is a good fit for who you are and who you want to become. Take a tour. Talk to students and faculty. Ask any questions you have about admissions and financial aid. And of course, take some time to explore our beautiful campus.

BURLINGTON & VERMONT ARE THE BEST

Burlington is home to a number of great college options (there are more than 14,000 undergraduate students residing in this lakeside city during the school year!) and has been called the best college town in America. Vermont is a nature lover’s paradise with more than 900 miles of trails to ski, ride, bike, hike and explore.
